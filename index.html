We are Alex and Alina Andronov

We live in Veliky Novgorod with our child and serve in our local church community.
Our focus today is not international missions, but faithful service where we are placed — building people, strengthening the church, and investing into the next generation. We believe ministry is not only what happens on stage, but also what happens in everyday life with people.

Our ministry

Alex is involved in worship and youth ministry.
Alex:
  • Worship team leadership (22 years in worship ministry, 10 years as a leader)
  • Youth ministry
  • Mentorship and discipleship
  • Music education (piano, guitar, drums, trumpet, electric guitar)
Alina:
  • Graphic design and visual identity for the church
  • Creative support for ministry and events

Work with children and youth

Alongside church ministry, Alex is deeply involved in social work with children and teenagers in Veliky Novgorod.
This includes:
  • Free music education for children who cannot afford lessons
  • Teaching multiple instruments in a church environment
  • Mentorship for teenagers
  • Work with children from difficult or dysfunctional families
  • Participation in events for children with disabilities (music and practical support)
Many of these children would not have access to music education without this support. We also work with teenagers from challenging backgrounds, investing time into mentoring and personal guidance.

Why support is needed

Our church is small and does not have the financial capacity to support full-time social ministry.
Support would allow Alex to:
  • Continue free music education for children
  • Spend more time mentoring teenagers
  • Participate in disability and social events
  • Develop long-term work with youth
We separate church ministry (internally supported) and social-educational work (needs external support).

Our journey

Alex was born in Yekaterinburg and has served in worship ministry for over 22 years.
He studied and served with Youth With A Mission (YWAM) and Discipleship Training School (DTS), which shaped his approach to discipleship and ministry. After a season of international missions, our family was led to Veliky Novgorod, where we now live and serve.

Mentorship

Beyond music teaching, Alex focuses on long-term mentorship.
He builds deep relationships with students, guiding them in discipline, personal growth, and values. This work requires significant time and emotional investment but leads to long-term transformation in young lives.

Accountability

We are committed to transparency.
Supporters receive regular email reports about:
  • Work with children and youth
  • Events and social activities
  • Use of financial support
  • Ministry progress
We believe accountability is essential in any form of ministry and support.
